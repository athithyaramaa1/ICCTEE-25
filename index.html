International Conference on Computational Testing and Evaluation of Engineering (ICCTEE - 25)

The International Conference on Computational Testing and Evaluation of Engineering will be organized from 31st July 2025 to 2nd August 2025 in Kalasalingam Academy of Research and Education( Deemed to be University), India. The conference is organized with the objective of bringing together innovative scientists, professors, research scholars, students and industrial experts in the field of Computing, Communication and Engineering to a common forum.
